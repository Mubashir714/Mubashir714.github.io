I served as a **Technical Reviewer** for the [34th ACM International Conference on User Modeling, Adaptation and Personalization (UMAP)](https://www.um.org/umap2026/), held in Sweden, one of the leading international venues for research on personalization, adaptive systems, and user modeling.

As part of the review committee, I evaluated peer-submitted research papers on:
- Smart home personalization systems.
- Structured conversational benchmarking methodologies.

My review process focused on assessing each submission's **methodology rigor, dataset quality, comparisons against state-of-the-art baselines, and ablation analysis**, providing constructive, evidence-based feedback to help authors strengthen their contributions ahead of publication.

This experience deepened my understanding of how personalization research is evaluated at a top-tier international venue, and sharpened my own approach to designing reproducible, well-benchmarked experiments in my own project work.
