I served as a **3x Section Leader(Trainer) for Stanford's Code in Place**, an online adaptation of Stanford's CS106A introductory programming course, teaching Python fundamentals to a global cohort of international students.

**My responsibilities included**:
- Teaching console and graphics programming, and control flow concepts to beginner programmers.
- Guiding students through their first experience with code, helping them build confidence early in their programming journey.
- Leading weekly sections for **6 weeks** each year across multiple cohorts between 2025 and 2026, supporting students from differenet educational backgrounds worldwide.
