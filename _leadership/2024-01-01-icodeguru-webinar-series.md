I organized and led a series of 50+ webinars on global Computer Science competitions for more than 20 Pakistani universities, helping students access international opportunities such as:

- **UC Berkeley Calico Coding Competition**
- **Stanford Code in Place**
- **Harvard CS50x Puzzle Day**
- **Meta Hacker Cup**

These sessions focused on outreach and accessibility, helping underrepresented students in Pakistan discover and prepare for high-profile global competitions they may not have otherwise known about.
